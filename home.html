Overview

What is Genshin Impact?

Genshin Impact is a free-to-play, open-world, action role-playing game (RPG). Players take on the role of a traveler from another world who must find their missing sibling in the land of Teyvat.

Released in 2020, it features a massive fantasy world called Teyvat, where players explore, complete quests, and battle enemies using a unique elemental combat system.

What is Teyvat?

Teyvat is the world and continent that serves as the primary setting of Genshin Impact.

This is where the traveler will take his/her jounrney to find its sibling

What is the Traveler?

The Traveler is the main protagonist of Genshin Impact.

The game offers the player a choice of either of the siblings: the Male protagonist (Aether) or the Female protagonist (Lumine)

The traveler can wield the elements without getting a vision.

  • Sumeru → Middle East & South Asia
  • Fontaine → France
  • Natlan → Mesoamerica
  • Snezhnaya → Russia
